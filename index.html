Taste the True Culture of Sri Lanka

Pol Athu Bath Kade is a family-run restaurant located on the Anuradhapura - Thanthirimale Road. Known for authentic Sri Lankan rice and curry, the restaurant offers delicious buffet meals with a calm environment, friendly service, and affordable prices.
